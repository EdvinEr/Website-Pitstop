Goals of the project

  • The user shall be able to see the fastest lap times for every driver on a specific circuit throughout seasons.
  • The user shall be able to see a timeline of the fastest lap records on every circuit throughout the seasons.
  • The user shall be able to see a timeline of the constructor standings throughout the seasons or for a chosen season.
  • The user shall be able to see a timeline of the driver standings throughout the seasons or for a chosen season.
